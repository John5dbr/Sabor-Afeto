About Us

Welcome to Flavor & Affection, an experimental platform developed to bridge the passion for gastronomy with the exploration of modern front-end technologies.

This project was designed to provide a fluid and visually rich recipe search experience, utilizing real-time data from the TheMealDB and high-quality visual curation via the Unsplash API. The main goal is to demonstrate the viability of interactive interfaces, intelligent API consumption, and a user experience (UX) crafted for those seeking kitchen inspiration with practicality and speed.
